Research overview

Understanding how monetary policymakers' use communication (speeches + press releases) to:

  • respond to their political principals

  • develop credibility

Developed vs. Developing

Monetary policymakers:

Developed: tend to have established credibility and autonomy and may be using communication to protect it.

Developing: trying to establish these qualities.

How to estimate?

Defining features a priori

Non-parametric change point methods (e.g. Matteson and James 2014) require minimum state lengths to be determined a priori.

Topic modelling with Latent Dirichlet Allocation requires a priori specification of number of topics.

Attempts to justify assumptions

Guided by substantive prior knowledge + what we learn from the data.

Rule of thumb for change point: aim for the smallest substantively meaningful minimum sizes to avoid arbitrarily ignoring shorter clusters.

Rule of thumb for topic modelling: smallest number of topics without overlap.
